// ABOUT THE EVENT

WHAT IS ASCEND'26?

THE HACKATHON

ASCEND'26 is a 24-hour inter-college hackathon presented by the ACM Student Chapter, Saintgits College of Engineering. It brings together brilliant minds from colleges across the region to build innovative solutions, compete, and showcase their technical skills. Whether you're into web dev, AI/ML, IoT, or blockchain — this is your arena to turn ideas into reality.

THE EXPERIENCE

It's not just about coding — it's about collaboration, mentorship, and networking. Work alongside your team, seek guidance from industry mentors, attend mini-workshops, and pitch your innovation to a panel of expert judges. Walk away with prizes worth ₹20,000, certificates, activity points, and connections that last.

// GUIDELINES

HACKATHON RULES

Everything you need to know before you start building.

ELIGIBILITY & TEAMS

  • ▸ Open to all college students across India
  • ▸ Team size: 2 to 4 members
  • ▸ Inter-college teams are allowed
  • ▸ Each participant must carry a valid college ID

SUBMISSION RULES

  • ▸ All code must be written during the hackathon
  • ▸ Open-source libraries and APIs are permitted
  • ▸ Projects must be submitted via the provided platform
  • ▸ A working demo is mandatory for the final pitch
  • ▸ AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot) are permitted but must be disclosed in the submission.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • ▸ Innovation — Originality of the idea
  • ▸ Technical Complexity — Depth of implementation
  • ▸ Impact — Real-world applicability
  • ▸ Presentation — Clarity of the demo and pitch

CODE OF CONDUCT

  • ▸ Plagiarism or pre-built projects → disqualification
  • ▸ Respectful conduct towards all participants
  • ▸ Judges' decisions are final and binding
  • ▸ Participants must remain on campus during the event
