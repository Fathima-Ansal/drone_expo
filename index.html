About AeroQuest 25

Where Innovation Takes the Flight

The objective of AeroQuest 25 is to spark innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence in the world of drone technology through a nationwide platform that connects creativity with engineering. Organized by ACM Saintgits, this two-day Drone Expo celebrates the spirit of flight and exploration — empowering students to push boundaries in design, autonomy, and intelligent aerial systems.

The event aims to offer a complete and engaging learning experience that blends education, innovation, and celebration. Day 1 features a grand Drone Exhibition, followed by an inspiring Expert Talk and a lively Cultural Evening. Day 2 takes flight with the Drone Competition, demonstrating precision and design intelligence. Beyond competition, AeroQuest 25 strengthens ACM Saintgits’ vision of becoming a hub for advanced technical learning.
