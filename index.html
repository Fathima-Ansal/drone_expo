EVENT OVERVIEW

Innovation meets Elevation

THE OBJECTIVE

The objective of Ascend '26 is to spark innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence in the world of drone technology through a nationwide platform that connects creativity with engineering. Organized by ACM Saintgits, this two-day Drone Expo celebrates the spirit of flight and exploration — empowering students to push boundaries in design, autonomy, and intelligent aerial systems.

THE EXPERIENCE

The event aims to offer a complete and engaging learning experience that blends education, innovation, and celebration. Day 1 features a grand Drone Exhibition, followed by an inspiring Expert Talk and a lively Cultural Evening. Day 2 takes flight with the Drone Competition, demonstrating precision and design intelligence. Beyond competition, Ascend '26 strengthens ACM Saintgits’ vision of becoming a hub for advanced learning.

MISSION PROTOCOLS

Official rulebook extracted from command archives.

GENERAL RULES

  • DRONE TYPE Custom-built, Autonomous AI Drone, with Max Weight less than 5 KG(including all batteries/payloads) and Max Frame Size under 500 mm (Diagonal Motor-to-Motor). NO Commercial-Off-The-Shelf (COTS) race/photography drones.
  • CONTROL Fully Autonomous. No manual input after launch. Telemetry allowed for monitoring only.
  • SAFETY Failsafe (RTL) mechanism required. Safety pilot with RC controller mandatory for emergencies.

MISSION STAGES

01

Autonomous Takeoff: Reach altitude of 15m - 20m from Home Position (H).

02

Navigation: Fly autonomously to Target Coordinates (T) maintaining 15-20m altitude.

03

Target Recog: AI vision must identify the RED pallet and initiate precision landing.

04

Dwell Time: Remain stationary on Red pallet for exactly 10 seconds.

05

RTH: Auto-return to Home (H) and land. Timer stops on disarm.

SCORING CRITERIA

  • WINNER METRIC The team with the Minimal Total Mission Time wins.
  • TIMING START Moment motors arm/spin for Stage 1 takeoff.
  • TIMING STOP Moment drone lands and disarms at Home (H) after Stage 5.

PENALTIES

  • Landing on Wrong Pallet +30 sec
  • Incorrect Dwell Time +5s per sec deviation
  • Manual Override/Takeover DISQUALIFIED (DNF)
  • Collision / Crash DISQUALIFIED (DNF)
